TMDB Box Office Revenue Prediction

Predicting Box Office Revenues Using Machine Learning

Introduction

The objective of this project is to produce a machine learning model that can help us predict the revenue for an upcoming movie. These predictions will help investors and producers to make good decisions about investing in a movie. We recommend a machine learning approach that can help producers to view these decisions from a technical and statistical viewpoint.

Background

Box office revenue predictions is an important field of study for the film industry. It helps producers, investors and other stakeholders in determining whether it is feasible to invest their time and effort into a project. Some existing solutions use very little data about the movies. Other solutions use NLP and reviews to predict the revenue. We will use a variety of machine learning techniques and determine which one is the most suitable for prediction. We will use genres, popularity, production companies, and other data points in our prediction. These terms are very common as most people are well accustomed to the film industry.
